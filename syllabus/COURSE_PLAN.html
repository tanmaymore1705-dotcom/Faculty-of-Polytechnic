How it works
Daily rhythm
  • 09:00–10:15  Lecture (75 min)
  • 11:00–12:00  Lab (60 min)
  • Mon–Wed: concept + 2 small problems
  • Thu: "Combine" day — one larger problem
  • Fri: recap + Friday mini-project (PR = artifact)
Submission flow
  • Fork the upstream repo
  • Sync fork each morning for new starters
  • Solve inside students/<username>/
  • Commit + push to your fork
  • Open a PR upstream — faculty reviews & merges
Assessment
  • 5 Friday mini-project PRs (graded)
  • Mini-Quiz 1 — D10 (loops + conditions)
  • Mini-Quiz 2 — D20 (strings + lists)
  • Mini-Quiz 3 — D30 (dicts + files)
  • Final mini-test — D39 (60 min practical)
  • Capstone PR — D40 (weighted highest)
