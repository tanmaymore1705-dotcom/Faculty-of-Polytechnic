>> 09:15 – 10:00 · Demos

Your 5-minute demo — what to show

Each student comes to the front. You have 5 minutes. Here is what we want to see:

  • Run your blockchain: show it producing and printing 3–4 blocks
  • Show the hashes: point out how each block's previous_hash matches the prior block's hash
  • Tamper: change one block's data — show is_valid_chain() catching it
  • One thing you're proud of: a stretch goal, a clean function, or something you figured out yourself
There is no grade for the demo. It's about showing yourself — and your classmates — what you built. That is the portfolio piece.
